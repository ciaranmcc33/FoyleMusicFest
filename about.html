About FoyleMusicFest

FoyleMusicFest is a brand-new music festival launching in St. Columb’s Park, Derry, in the summer of 2026. Created by local musicians and artists, the festival celebrates the creative spirit of the Northwest.

Showcase local talent, highlight emerging artists, and bring people together through music, identity, and culture.

Our Vision

  • Support homegrown creatives.
  • Celebrate the music and culture of the Foyle region.
  • Build a space where young people feel represented and inspired.

Community Impact

We work with local businesses, community groups, and volunteers to create a positive and sustainable event for the people of Derry, Strabane, and beyond.

Why We Created FoyleMusicFest

FoyleMusicFest was created to give young artists in the Northwest a real platform, with real support and celebrate the music and culture together as a community.

By building a festival rooted in community, culture, and identity, we are creating a space that allows our city to be seen.
